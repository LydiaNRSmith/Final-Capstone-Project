Our Story

3rd Street Printing is a locally owned, family run custom screen printing store in downtown Laramie, Wy. We offer custom printing services ranging from custom designed t-shirts, hats, awards, plaques and more. We have also started offering custom embroidery! Standard production time is about 10-12 business days.

We started our business in 2013 working out of our garage. Now we have moved our store location twice to accomodate our need for extra space and supplies. You are welcome to visit us in person at our store location at 463 W. Garfield St. in Laramie, Wy. Otherwise you can call us at (307)314-8832 or email us at 3rdStreetPrinting@gmail.com. Either way we are excited to hear from you!
